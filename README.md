# Towers-of-Hanoi
Super simple console Towers of Hanoi solver.

*Example:*

```
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 3
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 3 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 3
Move from Peg 2 to Peg 1
Move from Peg 2 to Peg 3
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 3
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 3 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 3 to Peg 1
Move from Peg 2 to Peg 1
Move from Peg 3 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 3
Move from Peg 1 to Peg 2
Move from Peg 3 to Peg 2
```
